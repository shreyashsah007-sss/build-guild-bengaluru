Overview

Build Guild is Hack Club's
1-day hardware meetup!

Running April 13th-19th in cities all around the world. Every guild is organized by a local Hack Clubber and funded by Hack Club HQ.

Who?

Teenagers aged 13-18. No hardware experience needed—just a curiosity to build things.

Where?

A safe, hardware-friendly venue in Bengaluru. Exact location revealed to registered builders.

What?

Soldering sessions, PCB workshops, project demos, and lightning talks with fellow hackers.

Cost?

100% Free. Hack Club covers food, venue, and basic hardware materials for all attendees.

For Parents

Safety & Supervision

Ensuring a safe and empowering environment for young makers.

Supervision

The event is run by experienced teen organizers with oversight from global Hack Club HQ staff. We follow a strict Code of Conduct.

Hardware Safety

All soldering and tool usage begins with a safety briefing. Organizers are on-site to assist students during technical sessions.

Non-Profit Backing

Build Guild is part of Hack Club, a global 501(c)(3) non-profit. Our mission is to provide technical education for free.

Communication

Registered attendees receive a dashboard with organizer contact info and exact venue details for easy drop-off/pick-up.
